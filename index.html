What is this?

The Frenzied Festival for Friends Facing Finals (6F) is your chance to make finals week a little less stressful and a lot more fun! We're funding creative projects that bring joy, laughter, and community to UCF during this stressful week.

Grants amounts range from $0-$25. There's a limited pool of grants, so if you have an idea, apply soon!
